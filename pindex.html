How We Got Started

Paws and Possibilities was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to provide loving homes for pets in need. It all began when our founder, Liz Carson, discovered the heartbreaking reality of stray and abandoned animals in our campus community.

Determined to make a difference, Liz Carson started rescuing and caring for these animals, finding them temporary foster homes, and working tirelessly to connect them with their forever families. The passion for saving lives and creating happy homes grew, and Paws and Possibilities was born.

Today, we continue our commitment to rescuing and rehoming animals, creating a community that values and protects the well-being of every furry friend. Join us in our journey to make a positive impact on the lives of pets and their new families.
